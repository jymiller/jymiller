enid Co-Founder Investment intelligence for private credit. What I’m building ▾

Enid turns the documents behind a private-credit portfolio — deliverables, covenants, risk, events, tracked across a deal’s whole life — into a governed, continuously-updated view every institution can trust. AI does the extraction; humans keep authority over every figure; and each number carries its source and confidence.

Why it belongs here: Enid runs the same governed-data playbook I bring to Snowflake engagements — medallion layering, data contracts, end-to-end lineage, and AI kept under human review. It’s that thesis proven by building a company on it: I don’t just architect trustworthy data platforms — I co-founded one.
